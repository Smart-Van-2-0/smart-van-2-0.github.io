Create your own SmartVanBox

The SmartVanBox IoT 1.0 is now available, along with comprehensive open-source documentation to help you build your own SVBox.

What is the SVBox?
The SVBox is a powerful IoT device, an essential accessory for any camper, van, or caravan. It serves as a smart control unit that assists travelers in maintaining their vehicles and enjoying every journey to the fullest.

How Does it Work?
The SmartVanBox is one of the three components that make up the SmartVan solution. Along with the SmartVan Mobile App and the accompanying cloud service, these three components work together to provide the user with a seamless digital travel experience.

What's inside?
The SVBox is installed inside your vehicle and powered by a 12V supply, similar to other camper services like lights and fans. It houses various sensors, as well as LTE and GNSS modules to ensure constant connectivity. Additionally, the SVBox is designed to integrate seamlessly with other devices in your camper, such as the SolarCharger. For a full list of components, refer to the SVBox's Specification page.

What Can I Do With It?
The SVBox is used in conjunction with its mobile app, providing a user-friendly interface to view and export collected data (e.g., energy monitoring, tank levels). The app also acts as a command and control center, allowing users to easily customize the intelligence of their SmartVanBox to meet their specific needs. More examples can be found into the FEATURES page.

Build It?​

Creating an IoT device from scratch can be challenging, but don't be discouraged! Our guides will walk you through each step of the process. To help you stay organized, we've divided the guide into six sections: from ordering the necessary materials to configuring the software, including 3D printing the case and assembling the hardware components.

If you lack certain tools, such as a 3D printer, find the nearest FabLab. There, you'll have access to the right tools and people who can assist you with the more complex steps.

Our documentation includes all the original files, so if you decide to customize your SmartVanBox, you can start with the models we've already created. Just remember to share your results with the community!

Don't wait any longer—open the BOM and start ordering the necessary components today.

  1. Order Materials
    Refer to the Bill of Materials (BOM) for the necessary components.
  2. Print the Case
    Download and print the 3D models specifically designed to house the ordered hardware.
  3. Configure the SD Card
    While printing the case, prepare the SD card that will be used with the Raspberry Pi.
  4. Assemble the SVBox
    Once you have the printed case and received components, assemble the SmartVanBox.
  5. Install Software
    Finally, install the required software, including the JOD SmartVan Distribution daemon.
