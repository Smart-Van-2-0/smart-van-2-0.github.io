Say goodbye to parking hassles with the Parking Assistant, your reliable partner
for finding the perfect spot. This smart feature uses the level sensors of the
SmartVanBox to help you park your vehicle on an even surface, ensuring a
comfortable night's rest without the worry of rolling around. Additionally,
Parking Assistant keeps you informed about parking ticket expiration and helps
you remember exactly where you parked in large lots, so you never waste time
searching for your vehicle.

<img className={style_feature.head_img}

* **Level Parking Made Easy:** The app uses the Smart Van Box level sensors to
  guide you to the best parking position, making sure your camper is perfectly
  level for a peaceful and stable rest.
* **Parking Ticket and Location Reminders:** Get notifications when your parking
  ticket is about to expire and reminders of your vehicle's exact location in
  large parking areas, helping you avoid fines and save time.
* **Seamless Integration:** The Parking Assistant works seamlessly with other
  features of the app to ensure you have all the information you need for a
  hassle-free parking experience.

## Practical Use Cases

**Level Parking:** Use the Parking Assistant to find the perfect spot in a rest
  area, ensuring your camper is level for a comfortable night’s sleep. Say
  goodbye to uneven surfaces and hello to restful nights.
**Ticket and Location Reminders:** After parking in a large shopping center, let
  the Parking Assistant remind you when your parking ticket is about to expire
  and where your vehicle is located, so you can avoid fines and unnecessary stress.


With Parking Assistant, parking is no longer a stressful task. Enjoy a hassle-free
experience knowing your camper is perfectly positioned and all parking details
are under control.
