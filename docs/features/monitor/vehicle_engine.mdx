SmartVan uses the OBD-II port to monitor the engine status of your vehicle. This
information can be used to keep track of various engine parameters, such as
temperature, speed, fuel level, and error codes.

SmartVan can also alert you in case of problems, such as a low fuel level or a
detected error code. This can help you avoid unexpected breakdowns and keep
your vehicle running smoothly.

Here some benefits of vehicle engine monitoring with SmartVan

* Peace of mind: SmartVan can help you travel more peacefully and serenely,
  knowing that your vehicle is being monitored.
* Preventive maintenance: SmartVan can help you identify potential problems
  early, so you can take steps to prevent them from becoming major issues.
* Cost savings: SmartVan can help you save money on repairs and maintenance by
  identifying and addressing problems early.
