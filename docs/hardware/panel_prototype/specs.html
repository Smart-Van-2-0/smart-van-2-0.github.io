Hardware - Panel Prototype

Specs provided

The following tables list the implemented Smart Van SPECIFICATIONS by this Smart Van Box. This allows you to verify that this box provides the specifications required by the FEATURES that interest you before you embark on building your own box.

For each specification, the corresponding firmwares are also listed. This makes it easy to understand the dependencies between the specifications provided by the Smart Van Box and the hardware it contains.

Remember that if you modify the configurations of the JOD Smart Van, specifically the struct.jod file, then the specifications provided by your Smart Van Box will change accordingly.

GroupSubGroupFirmware or DeviceDescription
PositionGNSSFW SIM 7600
The SIM7600 module can provides GNSS info from various systems (GPS/Beidou/GLONASS/GALILEO/QZSS).
PositionInertialFW Sense Hat
Using the QMI8658C and the AK09918 sensors, the Sense Hat can detect movement, orientation, and the magnetic field.
SensorsLivingFW Sense Hat
Sensors like temperature, humidity and barometric pressure plus an AD converter from the Sense Hat, provide environment related data.
SensorsEngineFW ODB II Scanner
A simple bluetooth ODB II scanner can be connected to any vehicle and get engine/diagnostic info.
ServicesControllersFW IOExp
With the AW9523B, the IO Expansion board can listen up to 16 buttons (8 by default).
ServicesActuatorsFW IOExp
With the AW9523B, the IO Expansion board can control up to 16 relays (8 by default).
EnergyStorageFW Victron
Victron's SmartSolar/BlueSolar series devices can provide info about the service's battery status.
EnergyGenerationFW Victron
Victron's SmartSolar/BlueSolar series devices can provide info about the solar panel charge.
EnergyConsumptionFW Victron
Victron's SmartSolar or BlueSolar series devices can provide info about the load consumption.
IoTBatteryFW UPSPack_V3
The UPS Pack with his own battery for the IoT block, provides info about the battery status.
IoTInternetFW SIM7600
The SIM7600 module can provide the mobile connectivity and his status.
IoTAccessPointFW RPi_Access Point
Use the onboard RPi WiFi to provide a local WiFi network.
IoTMonitorFW RPi_Monitor
Keep track of the RPi load status.
