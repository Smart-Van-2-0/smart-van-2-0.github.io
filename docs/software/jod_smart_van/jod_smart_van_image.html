Links

    This image represent the firmware to flash on the main Raspberry into the IoT Box. It a minimal RaspbianOS distribution plus the JOD Smart Van Distribution.

    In order to flash it on your RaspberryPi, please download latest image; then use the RaspberryPi Imager Utility to flash it on the SD Card for your RaspberryPi.
