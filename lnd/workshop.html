Build Your SmartVanBox with Expert Help

Join a Workshop Near You!



Want to create your own SmartVanBox but prefer some hands-on guidance? Our workshops are perfect for anyone looking to learn while building. Whether you’re a camper enthusiast or a tech novice, our project maintainers will guide you through the process of assembling, configuring, and personalizing your very own SmartVanBox.

With all the tools and components provided, you'll walk out with a fully functional SmartVanBox ready to be installed in your vehicle. Plus, with an additional fee (materials cost), you can take home the device you build!

For FabLabs & MakerSpaces​

Are you a fablab or makerspace looking to organize a SmartVanBox workshop? Hosting a workshop is a great way to bring the maker community together and introduce them to the world of smart vehicles and open-source innovation.

We provide the resources, guidance, and support for you to host your own SmartVanBox workshop. By partnering with us, your space can be the next hub for this exciting project.

  • Full Workshop Kit: We provide all the necessary materials, guides, and support.
  • Hands-On Learning: Engage your community in a fun, educational experience.
  • Flexible Options: Tailor the workshop to suit your space and participant level.

Contact us for more details, and together we can plan and organize a new workshop in your space! Let’s inspire creativity and innovation on the road!
