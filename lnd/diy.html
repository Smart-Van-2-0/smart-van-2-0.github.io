Smart Van Box DIY

Building your own SmartVanBox has never been easier



With the SmartVanBox, you're not just getting a tool, you're gaining full control over your vehicle's smart systems. Whether you're a tech enthusiast, a DIY lover, or an adventurer looking to enhance your camper experience, building your own SmartVanBox has never been easier.

Follow our step-by-step tutorials, order the required components, and create a smart assistant tailored to your needs.



What You Need​

Building your SmartVanBox requires a few essential components and tools. Don’t worry, we've made it simple for you. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • A list of all necessary components
  • Tools required such as a 3D printer

Latest list of components and tools can be found into the SVBox 1.0 > Resources page



Step-by-Step Tutorial​

Our detailed tutorials walk you through every step, from assembling the hardware to configuring the software. Remember to plan ahead – some components may take a few days to arrive, and 3D printing can take up to 20 hours. The earlier you start, the sooner you'll be on your way!

  • Print the Case - Get the 3D models and print them
  • SD Card Setup - Install the software that powers your SmartVanBox.
  • Assembly the box - Learn how to put together the parts.
  • Install the software

More tutorials can be found into the SV Box 1.0 > Guides and JOD Smart Van > Guides pages.



Personalize Your Experience​

The SmartVanBox is open-source, so you can customize it as much as you want. Add new features, connect additional sensors, or tweak the software to suit your travel style. We provide all the tools and guidance needed to make it your own.

Check out the JOD Smart Van Distribution's guides to get started customizing your SmartVanBox.

SmartVanBox is a collaborative project. Share your progress, learn from others, and contribute to the growing community of SmartVanBox builders. You’re not just creating a device – you’re joining a movement of open-source innovation on the road.

Ready to start building your own SmartVanBox? Order the components, follow the tutorials, and hit the road with your new smart travel companion.
