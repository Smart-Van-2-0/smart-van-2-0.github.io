## Personalize Your Experience

The SmartVanBox is open-source, so you can customize it as much as you want. Add
new features, connect additional sensors, or tweak the software to suit your
travel style. We provide all the tools and guidance needed to make it your own.

Check out the JOD Smart Van Distribution's [guides](/docs/software/jod_smart_van/jod_smart_van/guides)
to get started customizing your SmartVanBox.
