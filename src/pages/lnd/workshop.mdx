Want to create your own SmartVanBox but prefer some hands-on guidance? Our
workshops are perfect for anyone looking to **learn while building**. Whether
you’re a camper enthusiast or a tech novice, **our project maintainers will guide
you** through the process of assembling, configuring, and personalizing your
very own SmartVanBox.

With all the tools and components provided, you'll walk out with a fully
functional SmartVanBox ready to be installed in your vehicle. Plus, with an
additional fee (materials cost), you can take home the device you build!
